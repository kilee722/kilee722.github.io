About Me
Hello, my name is Ki Min and I am a data scientist. I hold a Bachelor's degree in science (BSc. Cell Biology, University of British Columbia, Vancouver, BC) and Master's degree in science (MSc. Business Analytics, Seattle University, Seattle, WA).
After working as a researcher for years, I became extremely intrigued with data science. Data science is playing a significant role in every field. If it is used properly, it can lead to good decision-making. Finding patterns and stories hidden in data nobody else sees are extremely fascinating to me.
I am currently working as a research data analysis assistant at Seattle University College of Nursing. As a member of the research team, I investigate risk factors for diabetes from population health survey data of California by data visualization and predictive model construction using machine learning algorithms.
